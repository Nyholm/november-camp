Call for papers

We welcome talks on any topic related to Symfony, modern php, BDD/TDD, dev-ops and more.

Submit your session proposal today! We promise we'll give it a fair consideration. We'll anonymize all Call for Papers proposals before selecting them, in order to give everyone's proposal a fair chance to be selected without favoritism and gender-bias. Don't hesitate to submit more than one proposal.

You can give your talk in English or Swedish (at your own preference).
